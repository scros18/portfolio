## 💰 PRICING MENTION TIPS

**Don't mention exact prices in ads** (breaks Instagram ad policy for service ads).

Instead use:
✓ "Affordable rates"
✓ "Fair pricing"
✓ "Great value"
✓ "Competitive prices"
✓ "Free quote available"
